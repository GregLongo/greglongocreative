A New Look for Medical Career Training

I was asked to do a refresh and redesign for the website of medical career trainig school. While retaining the content and branding of their original site I offered a design that was not only sleek and modernized, but allowed for greater focus on happenings and culture within the school community.

Designing for Data Interaction

I was brought in on a project a large mobile gaming company to work with their in house product designers and UI programmers to come up with the layout and design of a dashboard interface for visualizing and analyzing in realtime the large amounts of data associated with their advertising campaigns. Following a Scrum development methodology, I worked closely with the team, rapidly iterating on visual mockups and and anotations for the developers, Invison click-through prototypes and a very basic bootstrap demo. Building on their previous visual motifs, I ventured to create an interface that reflected the futuristic yet playful essence of their brand.
